Donate

Would you like to be a part of our cause? Donate $12.50 and help purchase a months supply of food for an impoverished family in Mexico. Your donation raises money to purchase a bottle of olive oil, a bag of sugar, rice, beans, two packages of pasta, a bar of soap and a dozen eggs for each family.

You can also choose to donate $9.00 to help purchase one “tilma” blanket or four toys that will be distributed the first week of January.
